<p><ul><li>Fibrin Sealant</li><li>Collagen Sealant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biosurgery sealants market includes two primary types: fibrin sealants and collagen sealants. Fibrin sealants, derived from blood plasma, mimic natural clotting processes and are primarily used to control bleeding and promote wound healing in surgical procedures. Collagen sealants, made from collagen proteins, enhance tissue adhesion and support during surgical interventions. Both sealants play a crucial role in various surgical applications, improving patient outcomes by reducing complications and promoting faster recovery.</p></p>

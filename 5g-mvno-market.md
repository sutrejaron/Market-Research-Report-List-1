<p><strong>The 5G MVNO Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full MVNO</li><li>Light MVNO</li><li>Thick MVNO</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 5G MVNO market consists of three primary types: Full MVNOs, Light MVNOs, and Thick MVNOs. Full MVNOs operate with their own core network and can manage all aspects of service delivery, offering maximum flexibility. Light MVNOs rely on a host operator's infrastructure but maintain their branding, focusing on marketing. Thick MVNOs act primarily as resellers, offering services without extensive customization, relying heavily on the host's existing capabilities. Each type caters to different business strategies and customer needs in the mobile market.</p></p>

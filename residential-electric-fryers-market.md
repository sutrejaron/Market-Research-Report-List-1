<p><p>The residential electric fryers market encompasses two primary types: deep fat fryers and air fryers. Deep fat fryers utilize hot oil to immerse food, delivering crispy results but requiring more oil and careful handling. In contrast, air fryers cook by circulating hot air, producing a similar texture with significantly less oil, making them a healthier option. Both types cater to varying consumer preferences, with air fryers gaining popularity due to health-conscious trends and convenience.</p></p>

Hydrotreating catalysts remove impurities such as sulfur and nitrogen, improving fuel quality. Hydrocracking catalysts break down heavy oils into lighter fractions, supporting diesel and jet fuel production. Catalytic reforming catalysts upgrade low-octane naphtha into high-octane gasoline components, essential for meeting stringent fuel standards and improving refinery efficiency.</p></p>

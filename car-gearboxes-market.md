<p><strong>The Car Gearboxes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MT</li><li>AT</li><li>AMT</li><li>CVT</li><li>DCT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car gearbox market features several key types: </p><p>1. **Manual Transmission (MT)**: Drivers manually shift gears, offering control and engagement.</p><p>2. **Automatic Transmission (AT)**: Gear shifts occur automatically, providing ease of use.</p><p>3. **Automated Manual Transmission (AMT)**: A blend of manual and automatic, allowing automatic gear shifting while retaining a clutch.</p><p>4. **Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**: Offers a smooth, infinite gear ratio without distinct shifts.</p><p>5. **Dual-Clutch Transmission (DCT)**: Combines two gear sets for rapid, seamless shifting, enhancing performance. Each type caters to different driver preferences and performance needs.</p></p>

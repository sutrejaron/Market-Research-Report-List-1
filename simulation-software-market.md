<p><p>The simulation software market is primarily divided into two types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based simulation software allows users to access tools and resources over the internet, facilitating scalability, collaboration, and reduced hardware costs. In contrast, on-premises software is installed locally on user machines or servers, offering greater control over data security and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. Both types cater to different organizational needs, with cloud solutions favored for flexibility and on-premises options valued for security.</p></p>

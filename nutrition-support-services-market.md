<p><p>Nutrition Support Services encompass three primary market types: Oral Nutrition, Parenteral Nutrition, and Enteral Nutrition. Oral Nutrition focuses on dietary supplements consumed by mouth to enhance nutrition and support health. Parenteral Nutrition involves delivering nutrients directly into the bloodstream, bypassing the digestive system, and is typically used for patients unable to eat. Enteral Nutrition provides nutrients directly to the gastrointestinal tract via a feeding tube, ensuring nutritional needs are met for individuals with swallowing difficulties or digestive disorders.</p></p>

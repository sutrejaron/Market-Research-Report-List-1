<p><p>The Animal Health Diagnosis market encompasses various diagnostic types essential for veterinary care. Clinical Biochemistry involves analyzing blood and serum to assess organ function and metabolic processes. Urinalysis evaluates urine for signs of disease or infection. Immunodiagnostics employ antibodies to detect pathogens or antibodies in animals, while Hematology focuses on blood analysis for conditions like anemia. Molecular Diagnostics utilizes techniques such as PCR to identify genetic material from pathogens, offering precise diagnosis for infectious diseases and genetic disorders.</p></p>

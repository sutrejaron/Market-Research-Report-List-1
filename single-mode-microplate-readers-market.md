<p><p>Single-mode microplate readers are specialized instruments used in laboratories to analyze samples in microplates, each focusing on a specific detection method. Fluorescence plate readers measure emitted light from samples tagged with fluorescent dyes, offering high sensitivity for low-concentration analytes. Absorbance plate readers quantify the amount of light absorbed by samples at specific wavelengths, useful for colorimetric assays. Luminescence plate readers detect light produced by chemical reactions, ideal for studying enzymatic activities and cellular processes, providing valuable insights across various research fields.</p></p>

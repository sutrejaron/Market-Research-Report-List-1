<p><p>The graphene thermal paste market consists of two main types: silicone-based graphene thermal grease and non-silicone graphene thermal compounds. Silicone-based thermal grease combines graphene with silicone, offering excellent thermal conductivity and flexibility, making it suitable for high-temperature applications. Non-silicone compounds use alternative binders, often enhancing thermal performance while being more environmentally friendly. Both types are increasingly popular in electronics and automotive industries for efficient heat dissipation, playing a crucial role in maintaining optimal operating temperatures in devices and components.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Thumb Splints Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Abduction</li><li>Stabilization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thumb splints market comprises two main types: abduction splints and stabilization splints. Abduction splints are designed to keep the thumb separated from the index finger, promoting healing of injuries or conditions like thumb arthritis by reducing stress on the joint. Stabilization splints, on the other hand, provide rigid support to immobilize the thumb, limiting movement to alleviate pain and promote recovery in various injuries or post-surgery scenarios. Both types are essential for effective thumb rehabilitation.</p></p>

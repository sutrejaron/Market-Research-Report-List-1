<p><strong>The Mass Flow Meters for Gases Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capillary Thermal</li><li>Immersible Thermal</li><li>Mass Vortex</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mass flow meters for gases are essential for accurate measurement in various applications. Capillary thermal mass flow meters utilize a heated capillary tube to measure gas flow based on the temperature difference caused by the gas's thermal conductivity. Immersible thermal mass flow meters, meanwhile, incorporate a sensing element immersed directly in the gas stream for real-time measurements. Mass vortex flow meters leverage the vortex shedding principle, detecting disturbances created by gas flow to calculate mass flow rate, providing high accuracy and reliability.</p></p>

Extreme Lateral Interbody Fusion (XLIF) accesses the spine laterally, and Transforaminal Lumbar Interbody Fusion (TLIF) approaches from the rear. Additional devices include other innovative fusion technologies to cater to diverse surgical needs.</p></p>

<p><p>The plasticizers market is categorized into three main types: low phthalates, high phthalates, and non-phthalates. Low phthalates are used for applications requiring flexibility and durability, often in consumer products. High phthalates provide enhanced performance in industrial applications but have raised health concerns. Non-phthalates, which include bio-based options, offer safer alternatives for health-conscious consumers and are increasingly popular due to stricter regulatory standards. Each type serves specific uses in industries like construction, automotive, and healthcare, balancing performance and safety.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Hydrogel Drug Delivery Systems (DDS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Physical Crosslinked Hydrogels</li><li>Crosslinking Agent Crosslinked Hydrogels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydrogel Drug Delivery Systems (DDS) are innovative platforms designed to deliver therapeutic agents in a controlled manner. Physical crosslinked hydrogels rely on physical interactions like hydrogen bonding or ionic interactions for stability, allowing for dynamic responsiveness to environmental changes. In contrast, crosslinking agent crosslinked hydrogels utilize chemical crosslinking agents to create stable and resilient networks. Both types offer unique advantages in drug release profiles, biocompatibility, and the ability to tailor the release of medications based on specific therapeutic needs.</p></p>

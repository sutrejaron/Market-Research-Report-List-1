<p><p>The PET preform making equipment market is categorized into three main types: hydraulic, electric, and hybrid. Hydraulic machines utilize fluid power for molding, offering high clamping force and energy efficiency, making them suitable for large production runs. Electric machines are favored for their precision, lower energy consumption, and quieter operations, making them ideal for smaller batches. Hybrid machines combine hydraulic and electric technologies, providing enhanced flexibility and performance, catering to varying production needs and environmental considerations.</p></p>

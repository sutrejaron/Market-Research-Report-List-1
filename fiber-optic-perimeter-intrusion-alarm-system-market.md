<p><ul><li>Positioning Type</li><li>Anti-zone Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber Optic Perimeter Intrusion Alarm Systems are categorized into two main types: Positioning Type and Anti-zone Type. Positioning Type systems utilize fiber optic cables to detect intruders by monitoring disturbances at specific points along the perimeter, providing accurate location tracking. In contrast, Anti-zone Type systems create a secure zone by detecting any interruptions in the fiber optic signal across a designated area, making it effective for larger perimeters. Both types enhance security by offering reliable, real-time intrusion detection.</p></p>

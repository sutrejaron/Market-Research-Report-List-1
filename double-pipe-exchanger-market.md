<p><ul><li>Reverse Flow</li><li>Cocurrent Flow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The double-pipe exchanger market features two primary flow types: reverse flow and cocurrent flow. In reverse flow exchangers, fluids move in opposite directions, maximizing heat transfer efficiency and allowing for better temperature control. Conversely, cocurrent flow exchangers involve fluids traveling in the same direction, which results in more uniform temperature profiles but potentially lower heat transfer effectiveness. Each type caters to specific industrial applications, optimizing performance based on efficiency needs and operational constraints.</p></p>

<p><p>The engineered wood market comprises various product types, each serving specific applications. Plywood consists of multiple wood veneers glued together, offering strength and flexibility. Blockboard features a core of solid wood strips, ideal for making doors and furniture. Hardboard is a dense panel made from wood fibers, typically used in cabinetry. Medium Density Fiberboard (MDF) is made from fine wood fibers, offering smooth surfaces for intricate designs. Particle board, constructed from wood chips and adhesive, is cost-effective for furniture. Other products may include laminated veneer lumber and oriented strand board, catering to diverse construction needs.</p></p>

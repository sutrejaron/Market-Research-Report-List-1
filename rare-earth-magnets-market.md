<p><p>The rare earth magnets market is primarily categorized into two types: Samarium Cobalt (SmCo) and Neodymium Iron Boron (Nd-Fe-B). SmCo magnets are known for their high resistance to temperature and corrosion, making them ideal for harsh environments. They are often used in aerospace and military applications. Conversely, Nd-Fe-B magnets are more widely used due to their higher magnetic strength and cost-effectiveness, commonly found in consumer electronics, automotive, and renewable energy sectors. Together, they dominate the market.</p></p>

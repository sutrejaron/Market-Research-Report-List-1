<p><ul><li>Cell Cast Acrylic Sheet</li><li>Continuous Cast Acrylic Sheet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cast acrylic sheets market is primarily divided into two types: cell cast acrylic sheets and continuous cast acrylic sheets. Cell cast acrylic sheets are produced by pouring liquid acrylic into molds, allowing for precise thickness and clarity, making them ideal for applications requiring high optical quality. In contrast, continuous cast acrylic sheets are created through a continuous pouring process, yielding larger sheets with uniform thickness, suitable for industrial manufacturing and applications where size is essential. Both types offer excellent durability and versatility for various uses.</p></p>

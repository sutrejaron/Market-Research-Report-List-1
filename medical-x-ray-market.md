<p><ul><li>Flat Panel Detectors</li><li>Line Scan Detectors</li><li>Computed Radiography Detectors</li><li>Charge Coupled Device Detectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical X-ray market includes various types of detectors, each with unique applications. Flat panel detectors offer high image quality and direct digital conversion, making them popular for general radiography. Line scan detectors provide continuous imaging, ideal for specialized applications like mammography. Computed radiography detectors use phosphor plates to capture images, allowing for efficient imaging and storage. Charge coupled device (CCD) detectors convert light into electrical signals, delivering excellent resolution and sensitivity, commonly used in dental and small animal imaging.</p></p>
